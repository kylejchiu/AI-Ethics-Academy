Why AI Ethics Matters

AI is increasingly integrated into hiring, healthcare, finance, education, and law enforcement. Without ethical oversight, AI can unintentionally discriminate, violate privacy, or make opaque decisions.

Understanding AI ethics equips you to:

  • Detect and mitigate bias in datasets and algorithms
  • Design AI systems that respect human rights and privacy
  • Explain AI decisions to stakeholders and users
  • Make responsible choices in deploying AI technologies
