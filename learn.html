What is AI Ethics?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) ethics is a field that studies how AI impacts society, fairness, accountability, transparency, privacy, and human values. It guides how we design, deploy, and regulate AI responsibly.

For example, AI used in hiring must avoid discriminating based on gender, race, or age. Ethical AI ensures fairness while maintaining performance.

Core Principles of Ethical AI

  • Fairness: AI systems should treat people equitably and prevent bias or discrimination.
  • Transparency: Decision processes must be understandable and explainable to users.
  • Accountability: Organizations and developers are responsible for AI outcomes.
  • Privacy: Data must be collected and used responsibly, respecting user consent.
  • Safety & Robustness: AI should operate reliably, avoid harm, and handle unexpected situations safely.
